Cookbook

A cookbook or cooker book is a kitchen reference containing recepies. Cookbook may be general, or may specialize in a partikular cuisine or category of food. Recepies in cookbooks are organized in various ways: by coourse ( appetizer, first course, main course , dessert ) , by main ingredient, by cooking technikqe, alphabetically, by region or countri, and so on. They may include illustrations of finished dishes and preparation steps; discussions of cooking technques, advice on kitchen equipment, ingredients, and substitutions; historical and cultural notes; and so on.
